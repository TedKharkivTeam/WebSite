Bookeye® 4 Professional Technical Data

This section provides you with the technical specifications and data associated with the Bookeye® 4 Professional book scanner. Speed, scan size, power consumption and environmental data can be found as well as shipping dimensions and weight. These documents are the most detailed summary of the technical specifications.
