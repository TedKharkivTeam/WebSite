Markets & Applications

The WideTEK® 36DS wide format duplex scanner is a valuable production device for the following markets and applications. This list is neither complete nor exclusive, there are more markets and applications than listed and it should be taken only as a general guideline. If you have a specific application that is not listed here, please check with your sales representative.

WideTEK® 36DS Markets

  • Media Control, Press Clipping Services
  • Universities, Libraries, Registries
  • Mapping Services, GIS
  • Copy Services

WideTEK® 36DS Applications

  • Scanning newspapers, periodicals, catalogs and magazines
  • Archiving blueprints and sepias
