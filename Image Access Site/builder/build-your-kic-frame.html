Five Year Cost Comparison

This graph shows hardware and consumables costs for the KIC fleet you configured relative to four homogeneous fleets: all copiers, all KIC BookEdges, all KIC Clicks and all KIC Bookeye 4s. Shown in blue is the approximate student and faculty time cost of copying and scanning on the various machines, also relative to your KIC fleet configuration. Student time value can be adjusted with a slider below this text. For example, high prestige schools tend to purchase bigger and faster KICs. They see their students' time as very valuable, and the savings from a single fast KIC vs. a slower book scanning system can approach $20,000 a year. This graph can also be adjusted for machine usage with another slider below this text. Additional adjustments can be made in the footnotes area.
