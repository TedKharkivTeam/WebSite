Wide Format 48 inch Scanner Markets & Applications

The WideTEK 48C wide format 48 inch CIS scanner is a valuable production device for the following markets and applications. This list is neither complete nor exclusive, there are more markets and applications than listed and it should be taken only as a general guideline. If you have a specific application that is not listed here, please check with your sales representative.

Markets for Wide Format 48 inch CIS Scanners

  • Architecture, Engineering, Construction, CAD
  • Utility Companies, Developers, Government
  • Copy Services, Reprographics
  • Mapping Services, GIS
  • Media Control, Press Clipping Services
  • Universities, Libraries, Registries

Applications for Wide Format 48 inch CIS Scanners

  • Scanning maps, color posters, display graphics,
  • Reproducing artwork, photographs
  • Scanning engineering and architectural CAD drawings
  • Production scanning of newspapers, periodicals and magazines
  • Digitizing documents from government offices and registries
  • Archive and copy blueprints and sepias
