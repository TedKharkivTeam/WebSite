Bookeye® 4 V2 Markets & Applications

The Bookeye 4 V2 book scanner is a valuable device for the following markets and applications. This list is neither complete nor exclusive, there are more markets and applications than listed and it should be taken only as a general guideline. If you have a specific application that is not listed here, please check with your sales representative.

Bookeye® 4 V2 Markets

  • Libraries
  • Archives
  • Universities
  • Information Facilities

Bookeye® 4 V2 Applications

  • Scanning newspapers, periodicals, catalogs and magazines
  • Digitizing files from file folders
  • Digitizing bound and stapled documents like contracts, accounts and documentation
  • Preserving books and sensitive historical documents
