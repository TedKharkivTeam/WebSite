Downloads & Tools for the 48 Inch Wide Format Scanner WideTEK<® 48C


One of the distinguishing features of our CIS based wide format scanner WideTEK® 48Cand all of our scanners is the unique Scan2Net® technology. A part of Scan2Net® is the ability to update the scanners firmware in a few seconds via the network interface. The firmware updates can be obtained through our customer service portal at any time and the portal will also always check, which firmware is suitable for your specific scanner.
