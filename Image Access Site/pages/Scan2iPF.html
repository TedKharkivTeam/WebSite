Scan2iPF - Direct Copy to imagePROGRAF Printers

The WideTEK® Scan to Canon iPF printer profile allows users the ability to print/copy directly to the Canon iPF printer of their choice, including the Canon iPF 650, 655, 710, 720, 750, 755, 760, 765, 810, 815, 820 and 825 printers. Now users can have a robust, productive multifunction system with scanning speeds more aptly suited for the output capabilities of the iPF series.

  • Fast copy to Canon iPF with the Scan2iPF option
  • Scan at 8.5 inches per second (12.7 m/min), matches fastest printer output
  • 1200 x 600 dpi true optical resolution delivers outstanding image quality
  • Perfect for production in reprographics, facilities management
