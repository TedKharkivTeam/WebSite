WideTEK® 44 Markets & Applications

The WideTEK 44 wide format CCD scanner is a valuable production device for the following markets and applications. This list is neither complete nor exclusive, there are more markets and applications than listed and it should be taken only as a general guideline. If you have a specific application that is not listed here, please check with your sales representative.

WideTEK® 44 Markets

  • Architecture, Engineering, Construction, CAD
  • Utility companies, Developers, Government
  • Copy Services, Reprographics
  • Mapping Services, GIS
  • Media Control, Press Clipping Services
  • Universities, Libraries, Registries

WideTEK® 44 Applications

  • Scan maps, color posters, display graphics, artwork
  • Scan engineering and architectural CAD drawings
  • Archive all large format documents including blueprints and sepias
  • Digitize documents from government agencies, utilities and developers
