KAT – Knowledge Access Tool

KIC KAT is the best way to promote the stacks to students in the digital age where demand for digital is nearly unquenchable.

With KAT Stacks View, students can use any smart device to "visit" your stacks from anywhere, even collaborate over which books might be helpful.

Then they can reserve a KIC system for scanning excerpts and go the library to get the book(s), scan selected pages, and maybe browse for more books, with the bibliographical records readily accessible on their own smart device.
